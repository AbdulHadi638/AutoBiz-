# AutoBiz – Week 1 Summary

Week 1 focused on understanding business workflows and how automation can replace repetitive tasks.  

Key learnings:
- A workflow is a repeatable sequence of business steps.
- Automationexecutes these steps automatically, saving time and reducing errors.
- Triggers start workflows (e.g., form submission).
- n8n is the tool used to build visual workflows without coding.  

Hands-on tasks:
1. Installed and ran n8n.
2. Created the first workflow with a trigger and action.
3. Drew a business workflow diagram.
