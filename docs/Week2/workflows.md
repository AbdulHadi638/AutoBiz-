## Workflow 1 – New Booking Request

Trigger: Customer calls or messages asking for a slot.

Steps:

1. When a customer asks for a slot, check the desired date and time.
2. If the slot is available, inform the customer and share the price.
3. Request advance payment (50% of the booking fee).
4. Once payment is received, confirm the booking.
5. Record the booking in the calendar/schedule.
6. Send a confirmation message to the customer.
7. Send a reminder to the customer 2 hours before the match.

Outcome: The slot is successfully reserved, and the customer knows their booking is confirmed.

## Workflow 2 – Booking Cancellation

Trigger: Customer wants to cancel a booking.

Steps:

1. When a cancellation request is received, verify the booking details.
2. Check how much time is left until the booked slot.

6. Release the slot so it can be booked by another customer.
7. Notify the customer about the cancellation confirmation and refund .

Outcome: The slot is freed, and the customer is informed.
