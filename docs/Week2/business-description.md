# Football Turf Rental Business 

# Business Description:

## Overview

This business rents out a football turf (5-a-side / small field) to customers on an hourly basis. Customers book a time slot, come and play for one hour, and pay a fixed fee for that slot.

The business makes money by renting the field for an hour or more for tournaments. 



## Target Customers

Our main customers are:

- Local football teams
- Groups of friends
- College and school students
- Corporate teams
- Regular weekly players

Most customers book during evening hours after work or school.



## How Customers Find Us

Customers usually find the turf through:

- Google Maps
- Social media (Instagram, Facebook)
- Word of mouth
- Local banners and ads
- Referrals from regular players

When someone is interested, they call or send a WhatsApp message to check availability.



## How Booking Works

1. A customer calls or messages asking for a specific date and time.
2. We check the availability in our schedule.
3. If the slot is available, we confirm the price.
4. We  ask for 50% advance payment to secure the booking.
5. Once payment is received  we record the slot in our calendar and the booking is confirmed.
6. We send a confirmation message to the customer.
7. Before the match, we also send a reminder.



## Daily Operations

Every day the business must:

- Review all bookings for the day
- Prepare the turf before the first match
- Turn on lights before evening sessions
- Ensure staff and refiree are present
- Turn off lights at closing time



## Payments

Customers can pay:

- At the venue
- Online or in cash

We record all payments and track which bookings are paid and unpaid.



## Cancellations

If a customer cancels:

- We check the cancellation policy.
- If cancellation is ater 4 hours remaining for the match ,then we only refund 50% otherwise full refund.
- The slot is reopened for other customers.



## Maintenance

To keep the turf in good condition:

- The field is cleaned daily.
- Lights are checked regularly.
- The turf surface is inspected weekly.
- Small repairs are handled when needed.



## Goal of the Business

The main goal is to:

- Keep the turf fully booked
- Provide a smooth booking experience
- Maintain good turf quality
- Build long-term regular customers
- Increase monthly revenue by maximizing time-slot usage
